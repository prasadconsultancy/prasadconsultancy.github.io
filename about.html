Jayanti Prasad Ph.D

With a Ph.D degree in physics from one of the top research institutes in India, Harish-Chandra Research Institute Allahabad, and having experience of working as a research scientist at National Center for Radio Astrophysics (NCRA-TIFR) Pune , and Inter-University Center for Astronomy & Astrophysics (IUCAA) Pune for a decade, Jayanti Prasad holds a unique position to advice young students and professionals in their mid-career to start/switch career in the field of data science & machine learning.

Dr. Prasad also holds a professional degree in science education (B. Ed), apart from having M.Sc in Physics, and cleared some of the toughest exams in India, such as CSIR-UGC NET (twice) and JEST. Dr. Prasad is a recipient of many national and international awards and research grants, such as Young Scientist award by the Department of Science & Technology (Govt of India) and the IUSSTF grant by the Indo-U.S. Science and Technology Forum. Dr. Prasad participated in many international events in Europe and USA and was one of the invited speakers for the Big Data (202) event which took place in Ancona (Italy) in January 2020.

From the very beginning of his research career, Dr. Prasad has been interested in scientific problems requiring management & processing of a large volume of data using state of the art computing technologies. Dr. Prasad started working on High Performance Computing (HPC) and parallel computing as early as 2002-03 when even the name of big data was not that well known. During his two decade long research career, Dr. Prasad worked on two parallel tracks - one related to science and another related to data and computing.

After managing one of the data centers of the LIGO Scientific Collaboration at IUCAA, together with carrying research, for three years, Dr. Prasad moved to Industry in 2018 as a data scientist with a software company (Embold Technology). After working for more than a year as a data scientist, now Dr. Prasad works as an independent researcher and provides Consultancy services to individuals and companies. Dr. Prasad has published more than one hundred research papers (check his google scholar page) and also writes technical articles on various platforms such as medium.
