A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step...

- A CHINESE PROVERB

We are living in the middle of an unprecedented disruption due to Covid-19. Schools, colleges and educational institutions are closed, business activities are frozen and the supply-chain is broken.

The current situation has made our dependency on information technology stronger than ever before due to enforcement of social distancing measures. Now we must learn to live in a world where on-line life takes precedence over the off-line life or physical presence but this should not stop us from learning and growing.

This crisis starts at the time when revolution in data science and machine learning is going on and this revolution will not stop - the job of a data scientist may still remain the sexiest job of 21th century !

There are thousands of online resources available free as well as paid courses which can help one to become a proficient data scientist but there are problems.

The problem with some of the paid courses is that they focus more on marketing than on the content quality and in the process of making their 'product' more 'user friendly' they can lower the standard up to any extent and compensate that with decorative and fancy certificates. The problem with free courses or self learning is that, it can take a very long time to become 'job ready' due to lack of right objectives, resources and the approach.

Here we offer personalized career guidance and consultancy services for those who want to start a career in data science and machine learning or want to switch from other engineering fields. Our motto is "help to learn and not teach".

We also provide consultancy services to companies & organizations who want to hire experts for their data science/machine learning division or expose their teams to latest trends & technologies related to these areas in the form of short training programs/boot camps.

Automation based on artificial intelligence & machine learning is the future and so find out which of the modules offered here is suitable for you and take your first step of a long journey with a clear direction.

Thank you very much !

Lecture given at the BigDat 2020 Ancona, Italy - January 13-17, 2020
