[PCCS-05] > Final Year Project

Course projects which are generally done in the final year of many degree programs (B.Tech, B.E, M.Tec, ME, MSc, MCA etc.,) play a very important role in connecting the academic training to the industrial applications. In most of the colleges the professors cannot give attention to individual students due to a very large number of students or they run out of ideas. If you are a final year student and looking for a short (less than 3 months) or long (3-6 months) project then this service is for you. You can consult your course coordinator if you can have an external co-guide. We will guide you from suggesting the idea to finishing the final report and preparing for an interview/presentation. In exceptional cases you may end up with a research publication also. The topic can be decided by mutual discussion and mostly will be in the areas of computer science, engineering, physics or some interdisciplinary area.
